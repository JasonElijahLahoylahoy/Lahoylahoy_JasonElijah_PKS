# Career Opportunities in IT Security

Career Opportunities in IT Security

## Overview
Cybersecurity protects systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, theft, and damage.

---

# Red Team vs Blue Team

## Red Team
Offensive security professionals.

### Responsibilities
- Simulate attacks
- Identify vulnerabilities
- Conduct penetration testing

## Blue Team
Defensive security professionals.

### Responsibilities
- Monitor networks
- Detect threats
- Improve security posture

---
# Cybersecurity Career Roles

## Vulnerability Assessor
Identifies and validates vulnerabilities.

## Penetration Tester
Conducts ethical hacking and exploitation.

## Security Researcher
Discovers and analyzes vulnerabilities.

## SOC Analyst
Monitors security events and incidents.

## Malware Analyst
Reverse engineers malicious software.

## DFIR Analyst
Handles digital forensics and incident response.

## Threat Intelligence Analyst
Tracks adversaries and cyber threats.

---

# Security Operations Center (SOC)

## Functions
- Monitor security alerts
- Respond to incidents
- Protect organizational infrastructure

## Three Pillars of Cybersecurity
- People
- Processes
- Technology
